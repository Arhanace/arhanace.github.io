Vendor for Consumers

Find the best street food around with Vendor.

Finding local street vendors and food trucks has never been easier

Street vendors are everywhere, but finding one serving what YOU want right now can be tough. That is why we have built a solution for this problem in the form of Vendor. When you first start the app you can choose to directly search for the goods you are looking for, wheather it be food, clothes, or more. From there, you can browse through a map or list of all vendors in that catagory near your location which can eithe rbe an address or your live current location. If you want to look for specific vendors or a specific item for sale simply type it in to the search bar and adjust the search parameters of where you want results to show up. After you found the vendor you are looking for you can simply tap "Get Directions" and you'll be on your mary way!

Skip the line with in-app ordering

Another dandy feature we included in the Vendor app is in-app ordering. In-app ordering allows you to view a vendor's menu and select items you want added to your cart. Once you've selected the items you want to purchase a confirmation message is sent to the vendro, if the choose to accept your order you can then set a pick up time and proceed to checkout. Once you are at the checkout screen you can choose to pay in a variety of ways including Apple Pay, Google Pay, and PayPal. Once your order is finished you will recieve a notification from the app, and then you can enjoy your food without waiting in line!
