Vendor for Businesses

Optomize Your Mobile Food Business With Vendor

Overview

Vendor is an app that allows street vendors and food trucks to easily broadcast their location to potential customers. It works by haveing the vendor set up a profile which includes the name of their stall, goods sold, menu, and description of thier business. Vendors can also link their website and social medai pages as well. In terms of setting up a schedule, vendors can either choose to set up a two-week schedule where they can schedule where and when they wil be everyday for up to two weeks, or they can choose for their location to be tracked live throughout set business hours of the day (this feature can be turned on/off at anytime). The app also allows users to get directions to your current location once they click on your profile, and if they love your goods they can decide to favorite your stall as well for quick access.

Location Services

Letting your customers know where you are at all times is a breeze with Vendor. We make it easy for you to boradcast your location to potential customers in one of two ways. The first is that we allow you to create a schedule of when and where you will be for up to two weeks, after the two weeks you will need to create a new schedule or update the old one. We do this because we want to make sure all vendor locations are accurate and that inactive vendors don't show up on the app. The second way for you to reach your customers is by broadcasting your location live during set businesse hours of the day. You can set what these hours will be on a day-to-day basis and can set a time for our location stream to automatically shut off so that people don't show uo to your house at night. Additionally, the location live stream feature can be shut on/off at anytime you like for your safety and convieinence

Ordering

Vendor makes it easy for you to accept online orders through our app. A prominent feature of the Vendor app will be in-app ordering. If you choose to enable this feature, your customers will by able to select items from your menu and order them online through the app. Once users place their order a notification will be sent to your smartphone or tablet where you can accept or decline the order. Once an order goes through your customer can choose to pay in either cash up-front or with several mobile payment option such as PayPal, Apple Pay, or Google Pay. These payment methods can be accpeted by anyone with a bank acocunt and do not require you to have and Apple, Google, Or PayPal account to accept. You can also choose to turn this ordering feature on/off at anytime depedning on how busy it is, and you can schedule when you want to take onlin orders as well.
